template<class _implementation_policy>
struct boost::outcome::v1_xxx::policy::basic_monad_storage< _implementation_policy >

A basic_monad storage policy which defines how the basic_monad is represented in memory.

The struct is at the base of the inheritance hierarchy, and simply keeps some storage for the monad.
